Nov. 2014

Inspiration

The ideas behind Aura arose from recognition of a disconnect between smoking hardware and the smoker's home and lifestyle, aesthetically as well as functionally. We started asking, how could a bong live comfortably out in the open in the home?

Jan. - June 2015

Design

In the design phase the team researched how traditional water pipes work, embedded ourselves in the ritual of smoking preparation and cleaning, and sketched solutions to address the pain points we experienced.

June 2015

Concept

Ideas were iterated upon, down-selected, torn from the wall, rehatched as new ideas, and stress tested with users until a final concept direction was chosen. Next, we paper engineered and aesthetically refined the details of this concept until we saw fit to present to the greater online community.

June - Dec. 2015

Prototype

Following an outpouring of feedback and great ideas from the online community, the team took a few steps back to evaluate material and manufacturing design considerations. Through rounds of testing with fully functioning prototypes we perfected the taste and smoke-ability of Aura.

June 2015 Preset

Manufacture

The process of selecting a manufacturing partner took place in conjuction with prototyping. Material sourcing and refinement of design details for tooling and manufacturing followed. The team has worked through four rounds of manufactured test models to get the little details that count right at the end.
