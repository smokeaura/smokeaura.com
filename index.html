DURABLE

A titanium bowl piece protects from accidental drops.

EASY LOADING

Avoid spills while loading. A wide platter surrounding the bowl catches excess smoking product.

COLD FILTERED

Smoke cools as it travels down the chilled downstem, before further cooling and filtering as it percolates in the chamber.

MODULAR

Replacements and upgradeable parts allow you to customize and grow aura.

INTUITIVE

Aura’s branching form provides a natural grip and angles the mouthpiece towards you to prevent discomfort while smoking.

IN CONTROL

The bowl piece is positioned directly in view to give greater comfort and control when lighting. Raising the bowl piece allows air to clear smoke through the chamber.

CLEANABLE

When dirty, Aura’s glass chamber is removable for cleaning by hand or machine.

STASH

Never lose the lighter, Aura provides a home within the mouthpiece for storage.

Nov. 2014

Inspiration

The ideas behind Aura arose from recognition of a disconnect between smoking hardware and the smoker's home and lifestyle, aesthetically as well as functionally. We started asking, how could a bong live comfortably out in the open in the home?

Jan. - June 2015

Design

In the design phase the team researched how traditional water pipes work, embedded ourselves in the ritual of smoking preparation and cleaning, and sketched solutions to address the pain points we experienced.

June 2015

Concept

Ideas were iterated upon, down-selected, torn from the wall, rehatched as new ideas, and stress tested with users until a final concept direction was chosen. Next, we paper engineered and aesthetically refined the details of this concept until we saw fit to present to the greater online community.

June - Dec. 2015

Prototype

Following an outpouring of feedback and great ideas from the online community, the team took a few steps back to evaluate material and manufacturing design considerations. Through rounds of testing with fully functioning prototypes we perfected the taste and smoke-ability of Aura.

June 2015 Preset

Manufacture

The process of selecting a manufacturing partner took place in conjuction with prototyping. Material sourcing and refinement of d esign details for tooling and manufacturing followed. The team has worked through four rounds of manufactured test models to get the little details that count right at the end.
